Shaped by nature, enhanced by style

Inspired dining options exalted by authentic, Greek-influenced cuisine are available whether guests choose to eat in the privacy
of their own terrace or at one of Mystique’s restaurants. Most of the vegetables and herbs used to prepare the refined dishes at Mystique’s
restaurants, are cultivated in the hotel’s organic farm in the village of Megalohori. Santorini’s rich volcanic soil combined
with the Mediterranean climate of the region, have proved nourishing sources for produce of unique and distinctive tastes and aromas.
